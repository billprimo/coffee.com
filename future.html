Are modular systems the future of espresso?

Read More

Champion barista John Gordon chats to Matt Haw about the rise of modular coffee machines and the pivotal role they could play in the future.

For more than a century, innovators have been pushing the boundaries of espresso machine design and technology. As such, modern examples are packed with a whole host of features that allow baristas to consistently pull perfect shots through even the busiest rushes. However, despite greater emphasis being placed on espresso machine energy efficiency, some in the industry believe that the real route to sustainability is to reinvent the espresso machine entirely.
